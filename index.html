About Me

Hello, my name is Paul Hyunjin Kim. I am currently a PhD student under Dr. Roger Crawfis at the Department of Computer Science Engineering of The Ohio State University. My current PhD research is focued on procedurally generating game content especially a maze. Before going to The Ohio State University, I received a master degree from the Graduate School of Culture and Technology of KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) in 2011. Also, I received a bachelor degree from the Department of Mechanical Enginerring of Korea University in 2009.

Research

My PhD research has been conducted on generating mazes based on topological constraints, such as a long solution path and lots of branches. Since game levels can be built on the mazes, this research can give designers more detailed control over level creation. Besides, I did some reserach regarding facial animation for my master desgree.
