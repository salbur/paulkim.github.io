About Me

Hello, my name is Paul Hyunjin Kim. Since this fall (fall 2020), I will work as an assistant professor at Bridgewater State University. Before joining to Bridgewater State University, I worked as a senior lecturer at The Ohio State University. I earned a Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Ohio State University in 2019. My Ph.D. research was advised by Dr. Roger Crawfis, and it was focused on procedurally generating game content especially a maze. Before going to The Ohio State University, I received a master degree from the Graduate School of Culture and Technology of KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) in 2011. Also, I received a bachelor degree from the School of Mechanical Engineering at Korea University in 2009.

Research

My Ph.D. research has been conducted on procedurally generating mazes based on topological constraints, such as a long solution path and lots of branches. Also, I did some research regarding facial animation for my master degree. Here is the list of my publications.

  • Paul Hyunjin Kim, “Intelligent Maze Generation”, Ph.D. Thesis 2019.
  • Paul Hyunjin Kim, Jacob Grove, Skylar Wurster, and Roger Crawfis, “Design-Centric Maze Generation”, The 10th Workshop on Procedural Content Generation 2019.
  • Paul Hyunjin Kim and Roger Crawfis, “Intelligent Maze Generation based on Topological Constraints”, 7th International Congress on Advanced Applied Informatics 2018
  • Paul Hyunjin Kim and Roger Crawfis, "The Quest for the Perfect-Perfect Maze", Computer Games: AI, Animation, Mobile, Multimedia, Educational and Serious Games (CGAMES) 2015
  • Paul Hyunjin Kim, Yeongho Seol, Jaewon Song, and Junyong Noh, “Facial Retargeting by Adding Supplemental Blendshapes”, Pacific Graphics 2011, Short Papers
  • Yeongho Seol, Jaewoo Seo, Paul Hyunjin Kim, J.P.Lewis, and Junyong Noh, “Artist Friendly Facial Animation Retargeting”, ACM SIGGRAPH ASIA 2011
  • Yeongho Seol, Jaewoo Seo, Paul Hyunjin Kim, J.P.Lewis, and Junyong Noh, “Weighted Pose Space Editing for Facial Animation”, Journal: Visual Computer, 2011
  • Yeongho Seol, Jaewoo Seo, Paul Hyunjin Kim, J.P.Lewis, and Junyong Noh, “Pose Space Editing of Blendshape Based Facial Animation”, Computer Graphics International Workshop 2011
